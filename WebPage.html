About Ringo

Ringo being one of the first playable characters in Vainglory, is a fleet-footed gunslinger and is suitable for newer players to help understand the basics of gameplay and positioning.

Abilities

  1. Achilles Shot
  2. Twirling Silver
  3. Hellfire Brew
